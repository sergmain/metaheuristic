OAuth2

- multi-layered authentication system that can manage logins and identity verifications for B2B clients as well as their own B2C customers.
- Each B2B client will have a unique 'realm', encompassing their employees and their B2C user logins. 
- implement role-based access controls and ensuring that authentication is specific to each B2B company within their respective realms.
- login flow that is compatible with mobile, desktop, and web clients.
